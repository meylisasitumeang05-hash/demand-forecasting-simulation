# Demand Forecasting Simulation for Supply Chain

## Overview
This project simulates demand behavior in a logistics environment using Python.  
It incorporates seasonality, promotion effects, and randomness to reflect real-world supply chain variability.

---

## Objective
To explore how simple forecasting methods (Moving Average) can support demand planning and decision making in supply chain operations.

---

## Methods
- Synthetic demand generation
- Seasonality modeling (sine function)
- Promotion effect simulation
- 7-day Moving Average forecasting

---

## Key Insights
- Demand in logistics is highly volatile
- Moving Average captures general trends but reacts slowly to sudden changes
- Forecast errors highlight uncertainty in real supply chain systems

---

## Business Relevance
Useful for:
- Inventory planning
- Demand planning
- Operational decision making
- Supply chain risk understanding

---

## Tech Stack
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
